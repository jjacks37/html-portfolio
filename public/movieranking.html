My Top 3 Films

The Long Kiss Goodnight

So cinematic, Awesome action scenes, classic story line, and Samuel L. Jackson. What more could you want?

Memento

One of those minding films that keeps you guessing until the very end. Wonderful acting.

One Day

From strangers, to friends, to lovers. A love story told across many years of the characters' lives. Plus, Anne Hathaway!
